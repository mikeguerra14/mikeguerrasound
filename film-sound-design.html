About This Service

I specialize in creating immersive soundscapes for films and documentaries. From dialogue editing and ADR to sound effects design and final mix, I provide comprehensive audio post-production services that bring your visual stories to life through sound.

My approach combines technical precision with artistic sensitivity, ensuring that every sound serves the narrative and enhances the emotional impact of your film.

Services Include

  • Dialogue Editing & Cleanup
  • ADR (Automated Dialogue Replacement)
  • Sound Effects Design & Editing
  • Ambience & Atmosphere Creation
  • Final Mix & Mastering
  • Dolby Atmos Mixing
