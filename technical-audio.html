About This Service

Technical audio is the bridge between creative sound design and interactive implementation. I specialize in audio programming, middleware integration, and creating efficient audio systems that bring sound to life in video games and interactive media.

From optimizing audio performance to developing custom tools and workflows, I ensure that your game's audio not only sounds great but also performs efficiently across all platforms.

Services Include

  • Audio Implementation (Unity, Unreal Engine)
  • Middleware Integration (Wwise, FMOD)
  • Audio System Architecture
  • Custom Audio Tools Development
  • Performance Optimization
  • Audio Pipeline Development
